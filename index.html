LINE-UP

SHMLSS

SHMLSS consists of two young city guys who started out in Utrecht. Their dj sets are diverse and they developed a particular sound. Resulting from the interesting mix of their personalities you can expect Disco cuts, Africa beats, Ambient stuff and Industrial House grooves. Always carrying the latest (or oldest) vinyl’s, they always find a way to surprise the audience and themselves with quality disco minded music.

Fouk

Fouk is a duo of two successfull producers, Daniel Leseman & Junktion, who bring house sounds infused with disco and a lot of funk. They are signed to the Heist label by Detroit Swindle, and have played Glastonbury, LoveBox and Output New York. These guys know how to make everyone groove to the beat. Make sure to check them out at Studuction 2018!

DEMUJA

This Austrian all-rounder knows how to make everyone feel the groove due to his breakdance history. After gigs in New-York, Paris, Berlin and a Red Light Radio set in Amsterdam, he will take over the turntables of Lijm & Cultuur. From disco to soul, he will take his best vinyl records with him just for us. His new album, Hidden Paradise, has been released last month, so expect him to drop some bangers.

Black Loops

Black Loops is a Berlin based Italian dj, that makes hot electronic funk and great fresh house. After taking Italy by storm, he now is playing all over Europe and we be playing Lijm & Cultuur on the 14th of June. Signed to the famous Toy Tonics label, he has been releasing some very good tunes. So expect Berlin Techno with a pinch of italian house, but most of all good vibes!

Chris Stussy

Chris Stussy is a Dutch DJ that brings serious grooves to the dance floor. After having played at festivals such as Straf-Werk, Loveland, Free Your Mind and Boothstock he will be playing Studuction this year. He has been playing and producing together with his friend Prunk for quite some time. After having Prunk at Studuction last year, it now is time for Chris Stussy. He has been following his own inspiration and produced some absolute gems these past years.

Samuel Deep

Samuel Deep, started out with his SlapFunk parties in Utrecht, will be at Lijm & Cultuur on the 14th of June. Besides setting up his own record label SlapFunk Records, he kept producing. He brings his very own sound with him, which is appreciated at home and abroad. You can expect house and techno, but most of all beats you can bounce to.

Wouter S

Wouter S is a Dutch DJ that brings a lot of energy along. Besides being a producer, he’s also owner of the 44BASS record label. He has been playing all over the Netherlands, and opened and closed for Fatboy Slim during his Amsterdam show. He gives you a taste of all kinds of sounds, including UK Bass and Garage. Don’t miss this one at Studuction 2018!

GEBROEDERS SCOOTER

Have you heard? De Gebroeders Scooter are comming over! The escalation Stars from Sint Michelsgestel shine like never before. After 9 successful mixtapes these shinning hotshots are on Total Escalation Tour! De Gebroeders Scooter only run hits, as much hits as possible! From the Spice Girls and the Backstreet Boys to Jody Bernal and Miley Cyrus: it will be one big party! We have to jump, entertainments, shuffling, french kiss, fall, get up and go again!!! ESCALATION!!!

JUAN SANCHEZ

One of the crowd’s favorite when it comes to techno music, Juan Sanchez! He has played Shelter often, and has given Berghain a taste of his music. He also has his own record label, which has seen releases by Laurent Garnier, Speedy J and DVS!. This man knows how to take over the floor with is powerful sets. He will blow the roof of, so make sure you’ll be there.

J’oak

J’oak, the man behind the legend. He has become a regular dj on several parties in Delft, and therefore he can’t be missing at Studuction. In his own words he plays: ‘’ Nice songs, guilty pleasures, disco, golden oldies, Top 40, forgotten gems ………...just a lot of good tunes!

De Klittenband

De KLittenband is a 5-headed group with only one goal: creating the biggest party of the year. From Justin Bieber till AC/DC, top 40 hits are played one after another . Screaming guitar solos, searing synthesizers, you will see it all. De Klittenband is a hitmachine which simply makes you want to dance.

Pokko Elective

It’s not about the one dj, Pokko is family. It’s about broadening your mind, exploring the unknown , it’s about hanging out and good vibes. Pokko brings people together and let them share the passion for music. So, you got something to share? Come and join us and expect things you never heard before, music ranging from the underground of India to the blasting percussion of the rainforest. Music to get you to a higher level, together we wil make it happen. We call ourselves Pokko, you too?

ABOUT US

STuduction

StuDuction 2018 wordt georganiseerd door de StuCo, een commissie van StuD Studentenuitzendbureau. 8 Delftse studenten met verschillende achtergronden zetten zich in om het vetste (studenten)festival van Nederland te organiseren. Bij StuDuction komen zowel internationale artiesten als Delftse talenten aanbod. Het beloofd een avond vol plezier, drankjes en dansjes te worden.

Check de: Facebook

STUD STUDENTENUITZENDBUREAU

StuD is hét uitzendbureau voor en door studenten in Delft. StuD biedt allerlei soorten bijbanen, echter gaat StuD verder dan het reguliere uitzendbureau. StuD stopt namelijk op allerlei manieren al haar winst terug in de Delftse student. Meer weten over StuD? Neem een kijkje op de website.

Meer weten over StuD studentenuitzendbureau? Kijk op StuD.nl

Huisregels

DOEN:

  • Legitimatie meenemen! Dit evenement hanteert een minimumleeftijd van 18 jaar.
  • Meewerken aan een veiligheidsfouillering. Dit is verplicht.
  • Let goed op je eigendommen. Berg waardevolle spullen op en pas op voor zakkenrollers.
  • Look sharp. Door hier te zijn geef je ons toestemming beeld- en geluidsopnames te maken en te gebruiken.
  • Volg de aanwijzingen van beveiligers en personeel altijd op.
  • Wees voorzichtig! De organisatie is niet aansprakelijk bij schade of letsel, tenzij dit het directe gevolg is van nalatigheid.
  • Bij het betreden van het terrein ga je automatisch akkoord met deze huisregels.

    NIET DOEN:

  • Eigen drank en eten, glas, blik, plastic flesjes, spuitbussen, vuurwerk en (huis)dieren kunnen niet mee naar binnen.
  • Wapens of andere gevaarlijke voorwerpen zijn verboden.
  • Fysiek of verbaal geweld, discriminerend of intimiderend gedrag, ongewenste intimiteiten en/of ander aanstootgevend gedrag wordt niet getolereerd.
  • Je mag geen kleding met provocerende, aanstootgevende of beledigende opdruk dragen of meenemen.
  • Andermans spullen stelen of kwijtmaken is verboden.
  • Professionele audio- en video apparatuur meenemen, flyers uitdelen en/of andere promotieactiviteiten zijn verboden tenzij anders afgesproken.
